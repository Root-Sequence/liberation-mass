# Why Liberation Mass

Most spaces today are not designed for being human.

They are designed for:
- consumption
- productivity
- control

This creates a mismatch between what people need and what their environment allows.

Over time, this leads to:
- suppression
- isolation
- overwhelm

Liberation Mass exists to provide spaces where people can:
- exist without paying
- express without performing
- connect without pressure

This is not about fixing people.

It is about changing conditions.
