# Spaces Overview

Liberation Mass spaces are designed around human states, not fixed functions.

People move between:
- arriving
- resting
- reflecting
- expressing
- connecting

Spaces exist along gradients of:
- visibility
- energy

Core zones include:
- Threshold
- Being
- Commons
- Rite
- Create
- Nourishment
- Mutual Aid
- Archive

There is no required path.

The space adapts to the person, not the other way around.
